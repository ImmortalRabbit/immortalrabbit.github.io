Story of Bilal

Hi, I'm Bilal, founder of Kynfluence.

In 2021, I began building a small yet passionate following of 5k people on Instagram and Facebook. Like many content creators, I faced the daunting challenge of monetizing my content.

I tried it all.
  • Brand partnerships had me hustling for months, only to yield a disappointing $2,300.
  • Desperate, I turned to selling merchandise, investing $5k of my own money, but ended up losing $3k after eight long months. I felt defeated and was on the brink of abandoning my content creator dreams.

However, in the midst of my uncertainty and frustration, a lightbulb went off. I realized my audience followed me for my expertise, and that's when I uncovered the true power of selling digital products.

With renewed hope, I created a high-ticket courseâ€”and it was transformative.

Over two years, I sold the course to over 220 of my followers, generating an astounding $300k in revenue while simultaneously managing two other businesses.

I want to share my story with you, not just as a tale of success, but as a testament to the power of determination and finding the right monetization strategy. I understand the feeling of being stuck and disheartened, but I also know the incredible potential that lies in selling digital products to your audience.

Together, let's unlock that potential, create a thriving business, and turn your passion into a fulfilling and profitable journey. Allow me to guide you on this path and help you achieve the success you truly deserve.
