Story of Bilal

Hi, I'm Bilal, founder of Kynfluence.

In 2021, I began building a small following of 5k people on Instagram. Like many content creators, I faced the daunting challenge of monetising my content.

I tried it all.
  • Brand partnerships had me hustling for months, only to yield a disappointing $2,300.
  • Desperate, I turned to selling merchandise, investing $5k of my own money, but ended up losing $3k after eight long months.

I felt defeated and was on the brink of abandoning my content creator dreams.

However, in the midst of my uncertainty, a lightbulb went off. I realised my audience followed me for my expertise, and that's when I uncovered the true power of selling digital products.

With renewed hope, I created a digital courseâ€”and it was transformative.

Over two years, I sold the course to over 220 of my followers, generating an astounding $300k.

I want to share my story with you as a testament to the power of determination and finding the right monetisation strategy. I understand the feeling of being stuck and disheartened, but I also know the incredible potential that lies in selling digital products to your audience.

Profitable Creators

Syllabus

  • Planning and mapping your MVC

    There is no point in building a course that your followers don't want. So you will learn how to test your course idea with an MVC.

    You'll learn how to create an (MVC) that meets your audience's needs and delivers value. You'll define your course scope, plan content and structure, and determine key features to set your course apart.

  • Crafting an irresistible offer

    You'll learn how to create an offer your audience cannot resist. We'll cover everything from understanding your audience and what sets your product apart to creating urgency and pricing strategies. By the end of the module, you'll have the skills and knowledge to craft a powerful offer that will skyrocket your sales.

  • Sell before you build.

    You'll learn how to create and sell your digital product simultaneously. Don't waste time creating something that your audience may not want. Instead, you'll discover how to build your course in chunks while generating sales and receiving feedback.

  • How to acquire customers (without ads)

    You'll learn how to acquire customers for your digital product as a creator organically. You'll discover effective marketing strategies, including social media content, email marketing, and influencer marketing. By the end of this module, you'll have the skills and knowledge to attract and convert your target audience into paying customers.

  • How to build the perfect digital product

    You'll learn how to build a comprehensive digital product, we'll cover everything from video content to resources and templates, action items, and more. You'll explore the essential components of a successful digital product, including how to structure your content, create engaging video lessons, and develop supporting materials.

  • Systems for scaling

    You'll learn how to streamline your creator business with efficient systems and processes that allow you to scale while maintaining profitability and delivering value to your customers. You'll gain insights on how to delegate tasks, build streamlined processes, and set up systems for tasks such as onboarding, payment collection, and reviews.

  • Team Building

    You can't scale to $300k in 14 months as I did without an A team. You'll need to know who to hire and where to hire them from. You'll learn how to hire appointment setters, graphic designers, commission-only salespeople, and funnel builders. Without an A team, you might as well admit that you will be stuck at $5k per month.

  • High ticket sales

    If you're planning to sell high-ticket cohort-based courses, you will need to know how to sell. You'll learn how to sell using a script, how to optimize your sales operations, and how to automate your sales process by hiring a commission-only salesperson.
